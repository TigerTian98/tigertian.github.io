TIGER TIAN

Is it narcissistic to have a personal website, or is it the exact opposite? After all, everything presented here is designed to impress you.

I’m Tiger’s personal website. It’s a pleasure to have you here. “Tiger” isn’t (thankfully) his legal name; he was born in the year of the Tiger, and the nickname stuck. 田 (Tian) is his surname—say “T-N” really fast, and you’ll know how to pronounce it.

What Tiger would like you to know about him, before anything else, is how much he loves music. He listens to music intensively and is a devoted record collector and concert-goer. He writes about music he likes and has a blog where he posts his articles (mostly in Chinese, his native language) and designs visuals for them. He creates original music and has composed, produced, and recorded a few mixtapes himself. I could go on and on about his passion for music, but my other sections probably do a better job in showcasing that.

Tiger earned his master’s degree in Music Business from NYU, a milestone that once felt out of reach. With a background in Computer Science and Interactive Media Arts, breaking into the music industry seemed like a long shot—but he made it happen. He believes his multidisciplinary experience blends artistic expression with algorithmic thinking, shaping projects that bridge both worlds. Some of his best work is here, including me (this website).

Some essential and interesting facts about Tiger:
· He was born and raised in 广东深圳 (Shenzhen, a city in Guangdong province), but he is not Cantonese;
· He has never had a drop of alcohol so far in his life, for no particular reason;
· He has heard “Into You” by Ariana Grande live twice (so far);
· His MBTI personality type is ENFP;
· (As you might have noticed) he uses he/him pronouns.

I hope you’ll discover more interesting things about Tiger on this site. Maybe you’re visiting from the other side of the world, or maybe you’re sitting right next to him—either way, thanks for stopping by. If something here catches your eye, don’t hesitate to let him know!
